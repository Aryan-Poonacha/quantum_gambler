The Quantum Gambler

Rules:
Like deal or no deal. Your goal is to pick the card that is the required card. But the cards are all in unmeasured, uncertain superpositions and are quantum entangled to each other, so opening one can change the contents of the other cards!
Choose the order carefully! Different orders for the same set will produce different outcomes, and it's all based on random chance.
Press F11 to play in fullscreen (recommended).
